Hi, I'm a college student at Santa Clara University who is trying to advocate for immigrants that come into the USA. I grew up in Soledad, CA and most of my friends parents had immigrated for a better life. Many of their parents worked in the fields and even some of my friends helped during middle school. I would simply like to spread awareness about the different reasons why someone would risk everything they have to live here.
